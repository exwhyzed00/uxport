About Me

Hi there! I'm a high-schooler who happens to enjoy designing products and experience based on the needs and convenience of user. I begun my journey at age 12 with web development when I decided to learn to add autoplaying video as website background— turns out, doing this and playing a welcome voice on page-load taught me a lot about HTML & CSS! Fast-forward to today, I’m a student of Higher Secondary School and have had the privilege of working Freelance with clients all around the globe, and several startups based in India and US. I’m a business-minded and driven designer who thoroughly enjoys engaging in all steps of the product life-cycle. My focus is on creating/designing products that look beautiful and function intuitively for the user.
